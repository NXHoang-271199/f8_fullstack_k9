Low and Slow

This week I am extremely excited about technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerde the food(usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is a precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In his book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter describes it as ultra-low-temperature poaching .
